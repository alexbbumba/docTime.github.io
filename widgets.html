Create Your docTime Account

From the opening screen, select Create new Account. Fill all the fields with the required information that is to say a working email Address or Your Contact number, Set A password for your Account, Agree to the terms and Conditions of docTime App. Select Sign up. An email will be sent to your email Account that requires a confirmation for that email address. After confirming your email Account an account will be set for You.

Select From the Top Doctors or Search for a Doctor you need

From the Home Screen you can either select from the available Docotrs and you can also search for doctor you need
